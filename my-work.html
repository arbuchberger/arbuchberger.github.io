Anne Frank Midterm

For my Literature in Translation 326 class, I wrote about the evolution of Anne Frank’s character and writing skills throughout her book/diary, The Diary of a Young Girl. The second mini essay describes the changes in Anne’s diary that she had made and her father chose for publication, and by analyzing different quotes, I show the strengths and weaknesses of the different editions.
